HOW IT WORKS

From First Click to Closing Day

Getting the right mortgage shouldn't be complicated. Here's exactly how we connect you with a licensed broker who fits your situation.

The Problem We Solve

Most Canadians don't know where to start when it comes to their mortgage. There are hundreds of brokers, thousands of products, and rates that change daily. Your bank gives you one option. A quick Google search gives you a hundred. Neither helps you figure out which one is actually right for your situation.

MyMortgageExpert cuts through the noise. We match you directly with a vetted, FSRA-licensed mortgage broker who specializes in what you need — whether that's a first-time purchase, a refinance, a renewal, or something more complex like self-employed income or debt consolidation.

Three Steps. That's It.

Step 1 — Tell Us What You Need

Click Get Pre-Approved or use any of our calculators to tell us about your situation. We'll ask for the basics:

  • Your name and contact information
  • What type of mortgage you're looking for (purchase, renewal, refinance, equity access)
  • Key financial details like property value, income, and down payment

This takes about 2 minutes. No credit check. No obligation. Your information is only shared with the broker we match you with.

Step 2 — We Find Your Match

Based on your needs, we pair you with a licensed mortgage broker from our vetted network. Every broker we work with is:

  • FSRA-licensed — fully regulated by the Financial Services Regulatory Authority of Ontario
  • Highly rated — must maintain a 4.8+ star rating on Google
  • Specialized — matched to your specific situation, not just whoever is available

You'll receive your broker's name and direct phone number immediately. No waiting for callbacks from five different companies.

Step 3 — Your Broker Goes to Work

Your matched broker shops your mortgage across 30+ lenders — major banks, credit unions, monoline lenders, and alternative lenders. They negotiate on your behalf to get you:

  • The lowest rate available for your situation
  • Terms that actually fit your life (prepayment options, portability, penalty structure)
  • A clear explanation of every option so you can make an informed decision

Your broker handles the paperwork, coordinates with your lawyer and real estate agent, and guides you all the way to closing day.

What Makes Us Different

  • We're not a brokerage. We don't sell mortgages. We connect you with the right person who does. That means no sales pressure from us — ever.
  • We vet every broker. Not everyone makes it into our network. We verify licences, review client feedback, and hold our partners to a high standard.
  • We save you time. Instead of calling around, filling out multiple applications, and comparing offers yourself, you get one qualified match who does all of that for you.

Who Is This For?

MyMortgageExpert helps Canadians in every mortgage situation:

  • First-time home buyers who need guidance on pre-approvals, down payments, and CMHC insurance
  • Homeowners renewing who want to make sure they're not leaving money on the table
  • Homeowners refinancing to access equity, consolidate debt, or lower their rate
  • Homeowners struggling with debt who need options to consolidate and get back on track
  • Homeowners with low credit or no credit who need a broker experienced with alternative lending solutions
  • Homeowners in power of sale who need urgent help to save their home or find the best path forward
  • Self-employed borrowers who need a broker experienced with non-traditional income
  • Investors looking for the best terms on rental or multi-unit properties

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to commit to anything?

No. Getting matched is free and comes with zero obligation. If the broker isn't the right fit, you're free to walk away at any time.

How fast will I hear from my broker?

Typically within a few hours during business days. In most cases, your broker will reach out the same day you submit your information — so keep your phone close!

What information do you share with the broker?

Only what you provide in the form — your name, contact information, and the details of your mortgage inquiry. We never share your information with multiple brokers or third parties.

Can I use your calculators without getting matched?

Absolutely. All of our calculators are free to use with no sign-up required. They're built to help you understand your options before you ever speak to anyone.
