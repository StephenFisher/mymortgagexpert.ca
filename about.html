Our Story

We've spent years in the mortgage brokering industry, and if there's one thing we've learned, it's this: finding the right broker shouldn't be this hard. Too many Canadians are left guessing — scrolling through endless options, sitting through awkward sales pitches, and still walking away wondering, "Am I actually getting the best product for my situation?"

That question is exactly why MyMortgageExpert exists.

We built this platform because we saw the gap between what homeowners need and what the industry delivers. The mortgage market is packed with products, rates change daily, and not every broker specializes in every situation. A first-time buyer has different needs than someone refinancing an investment property or a self-employed borrower trying to prove their income. The wrong fit costs you time, money, and a lot of frustration.

What We Do

We take the guesswork out of finding a mortgage broker. Instead of leaving you to figure it out on your own, we do the heavy lifting:

  • We vet every broker. Every broker in our network is fully licensed with FSRA (Financial Services Regulatory Authority of Ontario). No exceptions. We verify credentials, review track records, and only partner with professionals who meet our standards.
  • We know the rates. We monitor the mortgage market so you don't have to. Our calculators and rate comparisons give you a clear picture of what's available before you ever speak to a broker.
  • We understand your situation. Whether you're renewing, refinancing, consolidating debt, or buying your first home, we listen to what you need and match you with a broker who specializes in exactly that.

How It Works

It's simple. Tell us what you're looking for, and we'll connect you with a licensed broker who's the right fit. No cold calls from five different companies. No generic advice. Just a direct match with someone who can actually help.

  • Tell us your goal. Use our quick form or calculators to share what you need — refinancing, a HELOC, debt consolidation, or something else entirely.
  • We find your match. Based on your situation, we pair you with a vetted, FSRA-licensed broker who specializes in what you need.
  • You get the best product. Your broker works for you — shopping across lenders to secure the rate and terms that fit your life, not theirs.

Why Canadians Trust Us

We're not a bank. We're not a brokerage. We're the layer in between that makes sure you end up with the right person in your corner. Every broker in our network has been hand-picked, and we hold them to the same standard we'd expect for our own mortgages.

Your home is likely the biggest investment you'll ever make. You deserve someone who treats it that way.
